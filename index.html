ABOUT US

WanderLog that arises to help those people who need to simplify the planning of their trips from start to finish, whether it is a long or short trip for reasons of an urgent trip or little time to plan a trip for themselves.

About WanderLog

Our goal is to optimize and improve travel planning, taking into account key factors such as budget and destination.

We are focused on helping tourists and managing school trips to have a stress-free and memorable travel experience.

Our mission is to have a wide network of satisfied customers and as part of our vision, we seek to be leaders in the market at a national level.

About Us

We are a group of students studying Software Engineering at the Peruvian University of Applied Sciences..

WanderLog was born as part of our final project of the Open Source Application Development course.

Services

Our platform offers services for tourist trips and school trips

Travelers interested in planning school trips

People who are looking for student travel packages, whether for pleasure, vacation or promotion. They usually have very little experience in travel planning and therefore look for solutions that can help them organize their itineraries quickly and efficiently.

School packages

Companies that offer travel packages and deals, ideal for emerging companies, which allows us to be the perfect intermediary between the needs of both segments. On the one hand, there are tourist travelers, who want to put together a complete travel package, and on the other hand, there are travel agencies, those in charge of registering and publishing attractive packages for tourists.
